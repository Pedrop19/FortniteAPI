Fencing Fields is located on the southern side of the map, and this new POI is the best place to hot drop for several reasons. Despite being a small POI, you can move around quite easily at this location, taking cover behind the structures and vaulting over the houses. You'll also find the Nisha boss at this location along with several AI forces. Expect numerous players to drop at this spot, so it is ideal to equip yourself with weapons and shields before engaging opponents.
